Manual

This is a generative synthesizer. New notes will be generated every 2.5 seconds - unless in MIDI mode. Every cluster of newly generated notes will be referred to as a "generation" from here on.

Classic

The rules of Conway's Game of Life will be used to produce the next generation.

  • Any live pad with two or three live neighbours survives.
  • Any dead pad with three live neighbours becomes a live pad.
  • All other live pads die in the next generation. Similarly, all other dead pads remain dead.

Note that each pad's "neighbour" is defined by its numeric value. For example, pads 8 and 9 are neighbours, despite being on opposite sides of the grid.

Random

Each new generation will be unaffected by the previous generation. Instead, each pad will have a 10% chance of activating upon the next generation.

MIDI

Control the synthesizer using a MIDI controller. Though designed with an 8x8 pad in mind (Ableton Push 2, etc.), any MIDI controller can be used. Generations do not exist in this mode.
